Wuthering Waves

The story in Wuthering Waves is set after the calamity known as the "Calament" razed the world of almost all human life. Desperate to survive, the survivors forged uneasy alliances to rebuild some semblance of civilization from its ruins. Among their efforts to protect themselves came the Resonators, humans with unique compositions due to being fused with Wutherons, who can fight against the monstrosities that spawned along with the Calament. You take on the role of a mysterious newcomer simply known as the "Rover." Due to the circumstances of your arrival, the Resonators end up keeping you closely monitored. And gradually, as you connect with the world around you, the secrets behind your past and the world reveal themselves.

5 reasons why this game is my favorite:

  • It fosters exploration.
  • It has a multi-player feature.
  • Its map is beautifully designed, along with the characters.
  • It has an amazing and interesting story line that sparks my curiosity.
  • It has really kind developers and is somewhat a F2P friendly game.
